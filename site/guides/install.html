Installation

Get Operant running on your machine.

What You'll Need

Operant is free and runs on your own computer. You'll need:

  • Windows 10 or later
  • At least 4 GB of RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • Internet connection for the first time you set up (optional after that)

Download and Install

  1. Visit the Operant GitHub releases page and find the latest installer (it looks like Operant-Setup.exe).
  2. Click the installer to download it to your computer. It is about 300 MB.
  3. Once downloaded, double-click the installer file.
  4. A window will appear asking you to choose where to install. The default location is fine for most people. Click "Install".
  5. After installation completes, you will see Operant in your system tray (bottom right of the screen).

First Launch

When you open Operant for the first time, it will walk you through setup in plain language:

  • Choose how Operant should think (use a model you already have, download a free local model, or paste an access key).
  • It will test that your computer meets the requirements and is ready to go.
  • You will see a welcome screen and can choose to try a demo workflow right away, no setup required.

Checking Your Setup

If something does not work, Operant has a built-in health check. Click the Operant icon in your system tray and look for "Check my setup". It will tell you if everything is working and what to fix if something is not.
