Privacy and Security

How Operant keeps your data local and your workflows auditable.

Your Data Stays on Your Machine

Operant is built on a simple principle: nothing leaves your computer unless you choose it. When Operant works on your screen to learn and run your workflows, that information is stored on your device only. No screenshots, no recordings, no activity logs are sent anywhere else.

Choose Your Brain (Model)

When you set up Operant, you choose how it thinks:

  • Download a free, local model that runs on your computer with no internet needed (once it is downloaded).
  • Use a model you already pay for (like ChatGPT or Claude if you have an account).
  • Paste an access key if you want a different service.

If you choose a local model, nothing ever goes to the internet. If you use a cloud model, only the information needed for that one task is sent, and only when you teach Operant something new. Saved workflows run completely offline.

Run Completely Offline

After you have saved a workflow, it runs without the internet and without sending any information anywhere. Operant knows exactly what to do, so it does not need to think or reach out to anyone.

Passwords and Secrets Are Safe

Passwords, credit card numbers, and other secrets are never saved in your workflows. Operant stores them securely in your system's credential safe. If you teach a workflow that types a password, Operant will ask you to confirm it is okay each time before it runs. A workflow will never type into a password field without your approval, no matter what.

Sensitive Screens Are Hidden

If Operant takes a screenshot of your password dialog or a sensitive area, it automatically redacts that part before saving it. Your private information is never stored.

Activity Record: See Everything

Operant keeps a signed record of every workflow run. You can see when it ran, what it did, whether it succeeded, and what changed on your computer. This record cannot be changed or hidden; it is tamper-proof. Read it anytime to know exactly what your workflows have done.

Permissions: You Decide What Each Workflow Can Do

Before a workflow runs, you see a list of what it needs: which apps it can control, which files and folders it can access, and what types of actions it can perform. You approve these permissions. A workflow can never do more than what you approved.

Dangerous Actions Need Your Permission

Some actions are too risky to run automatically. If a workflow needs to delete a file, submit a form, or send money, Operant will stop and ask you in person. You see what is about to happen and you click "Yes" or "No". A workflow can never delete, send, or submit anything without your say-so.

The Kill Switch: Stop Everything Now

If something goes wrong, press a hotkey and Operant stops immediately. All mouse and keyboard input freezes in under 100 milliseconds. This is your panic button.

Undo: Go Back in Time

If a workflow did something you did not want, you can undo it. Operant remembers every file it moved, every clipboard it changed, every action it took. Click "Undo Last Run" and everything goes back to how it was.

See the Plan Before It Happens

Before any workflow runs, you can click "Preview" to see exactly what will happen. A numbered list shows every step. It is the same thing you will see after it runs, just before it actually happens. You can approve it, cancel it, or change the inputs.
