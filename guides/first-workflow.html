Your First Workflow

Build and compile your first automated workflow in 15 minutes.

What You'll Build

In this guide, you will:

  1. Open Operant and launch the demo mode
  2. Teach a simple workflow by demonstrating it
  3. Compile the workflow into a deterministic script
  4. Run the compiled workflow and verify it works

Getting Started

Launch the Operant tray app and select "New Workflow".

The Teaching Phase

Operant will record your actions through the accessibility tree and vision grounding.

Compilation

After teaching, click "Compile" to turn your demonstration into an invariant-gated TypeScript workflow.

Replay and Scheduling

Now your workflow runs in milliseconds at zero inference cost. Schedule it with cron, file watch, or manual triggers.
